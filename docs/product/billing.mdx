## Overview

**Settings → Billing** shows your current plan, deflection usage for the month, and lets you upgrade, downgrade, or manage payment through Stripe's customer portal.

## Usage tracking

The usage bar shows deflections used against your plan's monthly limit. Once you cross 80% of your limit, a warning banner appears with a one-click upgrade to the next tier — so you see it coming instead of hitting a hard stop mid-month.

If you reach 100% of your limit, AI auto-answering pauses for the rest of the billing period and new questions route to your human queue instead. Nothing breaks and you're never silently overcharged.

## Upgrading or downgrading

Click **Upgrade to [plan]** on any plan card, or use the warning banner's upgrade shortcut. Upgrades take effect immediately — Stripe prorates the difference. Downgrades and cancellations go through **Manage subscription →**, which opens the Stripe customer portal; downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

## Trial

New orgs get a 14-day free trial on signup (card required). The trial banner shows days remaining; your card is charged automatically when the trial ends unless you cancel first.
