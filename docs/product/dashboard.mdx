The dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of how your AI support is performing.

## Stat cards

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Tickets | Total support questions received |
| Deflected | Questions answered automatically by AI |
| Deflection rate | Deflected / Total — your key efficiency metric |
| Avg confidence | Mean AI confidence score across all deflections |

## Deflection rate

A deflection happens when the AI answers a question with confidence above your threshold (default 0.8) and the user doesn't escalate to a human. Higher = less manual support work.

## Quick actions

The dashboard surfaces quick links to the most common tasks: importing KB content, viewing open tickets, and running a simulation.
