Every question posted in a monitored channel becomes a ticket. Tickets track the full lifecycle from open → in-progress → resolved.

## Ticket statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open | New question, not yet answered |
| In progress | Being handled by AI or human |
| Resolved | Answer provided and accepted |
| Escalated | Handed off to human support |

## Filters

Filter by status, priority, and category using the dropdowns at the top of the Tickets page.

## Ticket detail

Click any ticket to see the full question, AI-generated answer, confidence score, source citations, and CSAT rating (if collected).

From the detail page you can:
- **Promote to KB** — turn the resolved Q&A into a knowledge base article
- **Escalate** — manually hand off to a human
- **Resolve** — mark as resolved

## Export

Download all tickets as CSV from the ticket list page.
