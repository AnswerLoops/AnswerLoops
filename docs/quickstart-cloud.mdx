title: Cloud Quickstart
description: Get AnswerLoops running in your community in under 5 minutes.
---

## 1. Create an account

Go to [app.answerloops.com/login](https://app.answerloops.com/login) and sign in with GitHub or Google.

## 2. Name your workspace

On first login you'll be prompted to name your workspace. This is for your team's internal reference — your community members never see it.

## 3. Connect your community

Choose Discord or Slack and follow the setup wizard.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Discord">
    You'll need:
    - A Discord bot token (from [discord.com/developers](https://discord.com/developers/applications))
    - The channel IDs where the bot should listen

    See the full [Discord integration guide](/integrations/discord).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    You'll need:
    - A Slack bot token (`xoxb-...`)
    - Your Slack signing secret
    - Your team ID
    - The channel IDs where the bot should listen

    See the full [Slack integration guide](/integrations/slack).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 4. Add knowledge base content

Go to **Knowledge Base** and import your docs:

1. Paste a URL (single page or entire site up to 25 pages)
2. Click **Import**
3. Wait for the crawl and embedding to complete (15–60 seconds)

## 5. Test a deflection

Post a question in one of your connected channels. If the AI finds a match above the confidence threshold, it will reply automatically.

<Tip>
  Start with a question you know is covered in your docs to verify the pipeline is working end-to-end.
</Tip>
