## Plans

Pricing is deflection-based, not per-seat. Every hosted plan includes the full AI pipeline — higher tiers add scale, insight, and support.

| Plan | Price (monthly) | Price (annual, per mo) | Deflections/mo |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hobby | Free | — | 50 |
| Pro | $29 | $23.20 (20% off) | 500 |
| Scale | $79 | $63.20 (20% off) | 2,000 |
| Enterprise | $299 | $239.20 (20% off) | Unlimited |

Hobby is the post-trial fallback plan, not something you sign up for directly — it's what a trialing org drops to if it cancels without choosing a paid plan.

Annual billing saves 20% off the monthly rate, shown as an Annual/Monthly toggle on the public [pricing page](https://answerloops.com/pricing). There is no separate Stripe annual price configured yet — annual is a display-only rate on the marketing page until the product leaves waitlist mode.

## What counts as a deflection

A deflection is one question the AI answered automatically with high enough confidence that no human needed to step in. Questions routed to a human for review — even if the AI drafted a suggested reply — don't count.

## Trial

Every hosted plan starts with a 14-day free trial (card required at signup, `trial_period_days: 14` in Stripe). Cancel any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

## What's included per tier

See the comparison table on the [pricing page](https://answerloops.com/pricing) for the full feature breakdown. Summary:

- **Hobby** — core channels (Discord, Slack, GitHub, Telegram, Email), website widget, knowledge base, bring-your-own AI provider.
- **Pro** — everything in Hobby, plus CSV export and the white-label widget (removes "Powered by AnswerLoops").
- **Scale** — everything in Pro, plus CSAT scoring, human escalation routing, simulation/dry-run mode, knowledge gap dashboard, and priority support.
- **Enterprise** — everything in Scale, plus custom AI model configuration, SLA + dedicated support, and custom invoicing.

## Approaching or hitting your limit

Once an org crosses 80% of its monthly deflection allowance, an in-app warning banner appears on **Settings → Billing** with a one-click upgrade to the next tier. If the limit is reached, AI auto-answering pauses for the rest of the billing period and new questions route straight to the human queue instead — nothing breaks, and there's no surprise overage charge.

## Self-hosting

The core platform is AGPL-3.0 licensed. Self-hosted deployments have no deflection limit and no billing integration required — see [Production Setup Guide](/self-hosting/production).
